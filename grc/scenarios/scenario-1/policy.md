# IT Access Control Policy

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish the rules for the granting, modification, and revocation of access to HaxNation IT systems and data.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and temporary workers at HaxNation.

## 3. General Principles
- Access to IT resources shall be granted on a "need-to-know" basis.
- Segregation of duties must be maintained where applicable to prevent unauthorized actions.
- Default access is to be set to "deny all".

## 4. User Access Management

### 4.1 Provisioning
New users will be granted access upon receipt of a formalized request from their immediate supervisor. The request must detail the exact systems and roles required.

### 4.2 Review
Access rights to all critical systems must be reviewed at least annually.

### 4.3 Responsibilities
The policy currently lacks explicit definitions of who is responsible for each step of the access lifecycle.
A formal RACI matrix must be established and appended to this policy before the next audit cycle.

## 5. Enforcement
Any employee found to have violated this policy may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
